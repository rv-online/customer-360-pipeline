# Operations

## What To Inspect After A Run

- `record_count` and `unique_customer_count`
- `health_counts` for retention posture
- `concentration.top_two_customer_share` for dependency risk
- `priority_customers` for the accounts most worth proactive outreach

## Useful Signals

- `value_band_counts` shows whether the portfolio is dominated by enterprise, growth, or self-serve accounts.
- `segment_profiles` gives average value and engagement by segment.
- `operational_notes` provides a concise narrative for stakeholders who do not want to parse the whole JSON object.

## Follow-Up Questions

- Which segment is overperforming on value versus activity?
- Which customer accounts dominate revenue concentration?
- Which customers should customer success or sales look at first?
